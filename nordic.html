Overview

Context

Nordic Bazaar is an upcoming fashion brand that wholesales to boutique retailers. The products are conceived by Scandinavian designers and manufactured by a production house in India. The designers and manufacturers have been working together for over 20 years.

This is a new joint venture, in which the designers are culturally aware of traditional techniques, and the manufacturers are forward-thinking innovators. This unique relationship called for a brand to match.

Process

The logo takes inspiration from each, the Scandinavian and the Indian cultures. The sea-faring mercantile history of India and Nordic viking ships provided an interesting nautical metaphor.

The type treatment is kept light and modern, keeping it minimal and Scandinavian. The recycled paper texture reflects the historic culture of Indian handicrafts. And the asymmetrical ship logo connotes movement forward without leaving behind the brand's cultural roots.

Summary

The simple elegance of the logotype was well received by the client and their customers, and this project gave me a chance to design artefacts previously unfamiliar to me, such as labels and hangtags. Branding for Nordic Bazaar also informed how I designed my own brand identity, and made me appreciate the authenticity that needs to be communicated in a brand within a very short amount of time.
